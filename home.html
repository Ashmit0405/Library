The IIIT Kota library is a vibrant hub of knowledge and learning, serving as an indispensable resource for students, faculty, and researchers. With an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources, the library caters to the diverse academic needs of the IIIT Kota community. From traditional print materials to cutting-edge online databases, the IIIT Kota library is committed to supporting the educational and research endeavors of its patrons.
